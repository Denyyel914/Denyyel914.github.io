The New XBOX SERIES S

Xbox Series S delivers four times the processing power of an Xbox One console and supports experiences up to 120fps. Xbox Series S includes 10GB of GDDR6 memory running at 224Gbps, compared to the 16GB of GDDR6 found on the Xbox Series X.
